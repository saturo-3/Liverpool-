Liverpool FC

Liverpool Football Club is a professional football club based in Liverpool, England. Founded in 1892, Liverpool FC has won numerous domestic and international trophies, including 19 League titles, 6 European Cups, and 7 FA Cups. The club's iconic anthem is "You'll Never Walk Alone".
